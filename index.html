    What sets us apart?

    The TCIS Advantage

    Child-centric Differentiated Learning
    Child-at-the-centre approach is in our DNA, wherein the teaching strategy is curated to meet each child’s learning needs. This differentiated teaching-learning facilitated by TCIS’ personalised learning ecosystem and infrastructure has immense positive impact on the child’s development.
    Future Oriented Skilling
    In a fast evolving world, education is no longer just academics but equipping students with the skills, mindset, and global exposure necessary to thrive as global citizens of tomorrow. TCIS stands at the forefront of this transformational shift in schooling sector.
    Strategic Global Alliances & Cross-Cultural Diversity
    First of its kind alliance of TCIS with distinguished global institutions enrich its teachers and students with immersive scholarly experiences and seamlessly integrate global best practices into TCIS classrooms. Its multicultural learning environment acts as catalyst for cultural and intellectual cross-pollination.
    Unique Blended Curriculum & Academic Audit
    Developed by top educational experts, TCIS follows its own enhanced pedagogical approach by combining the best practices of international curriculums along with CBSE and its effective implementation, constantly monitored by independent academic auditors.
    Multidimensional Development
    Physical and mental health, grit, focus, critical thinking, collaboration and creativity are vital ingredients for all-round success of children. To bring about these higher order outcomes, TCIS has a re-imagined its sports, arts and student counselling system and added new-age initiatives like entrepreneurship.
    Academic & Co-scholastic Accomplishments
    TCIS’ halls echo with names of ace talents both on and off the field. There are Ivy league grads, high-science researchers, authors with published works, genius designers, master musicians, young start-up entrepreneurs, visual arts pros and many more accomplished champions.
