    Infra @ TCIS

    The entire infrastructure of TCIS follows a purpose-built philosophy conducive for learning. TCIS campuses are large, with a lot of green lung space and are built with a character of openness. Our enriching campus infrastructure touches every aspect of learning – spanning from academics & research spaces to sports & arts centres – as well as every learning support systems – from safety & security to transport & canteen facilities.

    Learning Spaces

    Academic Infra

    Flexy Classrooms

    All TCIS classrooms are smart, spacious and well equipped. It is dynamically designed and flexible to orient for learning requirements like group discussion, debates, presentations and group projects.

    Learning Corners

    From Kids Activity Arena to Teachers Training Centres, resource rooms are integral part of TCIS’ active learning engagements.

    Resource Rooms

    We believe in the philosophy of ‘every space is a learning space’. While you find science corners and literary corners inside the classrooms, there are math corners and value-ed corners in the corridors.
