    About us

    Our Story

    The recent times have witnessed the world changing faster than ever with far-reaching technological advancements like artificial intelligence and pressing global challenges like climate-change. As educators with four decades of experience, we find ourselves reflecting on whether the prevailing schooling system truly prepares students to navigate a changed tomorrow.

    This introspection led us to envision and establish a network of world-class, progressive K-12 schools designed to equip students with the skills, mindsets, and global perspectives necessary to thrive as future global leaders. This required a paradigm shift from conventional schooling approach and thus, born TCIS in the year 2016.

    TCIS was founded and is guided by a diverse team of experts from both academic and non-academic fields. This cross-sectoral approach has fostered a culture of innovation and progressiveness, evident in every aspect of TCIS. While our unique educational model has earned numerous accolades, its true impact is best reflected in the achievements of our students and alumni.
